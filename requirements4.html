Retention Requirements

Students must maintain academic standards in order to continue in the Creative Technologies program. GPA requirements vary depending on the chosen sequence and must be maintained throughout enrollment.

  • Audio and Music Production – Maintain 2.50 GPA.
  • Game Design – Maintain 2.50 overall and 3.0 within major.

Standards for Progress

Students must demonstrate competency through an intermediate portfolio review.
